About Us

Why The Name "Beacon Of Strength"?

At Beacon of Strength Breast Cancer Foundation, we believe that every individual touched by breast cancer deserves to have a guiding light to illuminate their path. Just as a beacon offers direction and hope to those navigating through darkness, we strive to provide clarity and comfort to patients and their families. Our name reflects our commitment to being a source of strength and support for those facing the challenges of breast cancer. By shining a light on the importance of comprehensive care and unwavering support, we hope to inspire strength and courage in all those affected by this disease.

Our Mission

Our mission at Beacon of Strength Breast Cancer Foundation is to empower and support individuals affected by breast cancer through financial assistance, emotional support, and valuable resources. We aim to be a guiding light for patients and their families, illuminating the path to recovery with compassion and care. Just as a beacon offers direction and hope to those navigating through darkness, we strive to provide clarity and comfort to those facing the challenges of breast cancer.

Our Vision

We envision a world where no breast cancer patient has to face their journey alone. At Beacon of Strength, we aspire to create a community where every individual has access to the support and care they need to thrive. Our vision is to be a steadfast beacon of strength, fostering resilience and solidarity among patients, survivors, and their families. By shining a light on the importance of comprehensive care and unwavering support, we hope to inspire strength and courage in all those touched by breast cancer.

Our Founder

Vanessa Washington

Vanessa Washington is a dedicated educator who has devoted her life to teaching and inspiring children. Her passion for education has been the cornerstone of her career, as she has tirelessly worked to nurture young minds and foster a love for learning. However, Vanessa's journey took an unexpected turn when she was diagnosed with breast cancer. This life-altering diagnosis forced her to step away from the classroom she loved so much.
Enduring the challenges of chemotherapy and radiation, Vanessa's faith and determination became her guiding lights. Through prayer and the grace of God, she found strength and hope in her darkest moments. Within a year of completing her treatment, Vanessa experienced a miraculous recovery. Healed and invigorated, she returned to her calling in education, this time stepping into a leadership role. Her experiences as a survivor gave her a renewed sense of purpose and a deeper empathy for those facing similar battles.
Driven by a desire to raise awareness and support for breast cancer patients, Vanessa founded the Beacon of Strength Breast Cancer Foundation. This foundation is dedicated to providing essential resources, emotional support, and advocacy for individuals and families affected by breast cancer. Under Vanessa's compassionate leadership, Beacon of Strength strives to be a source of strength and encouragement for those fighting this disease, reflecting her unwavering commitment to making a positive impact in both education and health.
