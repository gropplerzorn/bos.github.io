Paint The Town Pink

The 2024 Paint the Town Pink is Beacon of Strength Breast Cancer Foundation's inaugural event, dedicated to raising funds and awareness for our mission. Held on October 26, 2024, this vibrant community gathering will bring together supporters to honor those affected by breast cancer through various activities and celebrations.
It's a day to support current patients, celebrate survivors, and honor the memory of those who have lost their battle with breast cancer. Join us for an impactful day featuring:
  • 5k Run/Walk: Register for our 5k and receive an exclusive event t-shirt. (Registration link coming soon.)
  • Red Cross Blood Truck: Donate blood and contribute to saving lives.
  • Free Mammograms: Benefit from free mammogram screenings available on-site.
  • Food Truck: Enjoy a variety of delicious options from our featured food truck.
  • Guest Speakers: Listen to inspiring stories and valuable insights from our speakers.
  • Fun Activities: Participate in various activities designed for all ages.

Breast Cancer Awareness Month: Paint the Town Pink Decoration Contest

Additionally, local businesses are invited to participate in our Paint the Town Pink Decoration Contest. Show your support by decorating your windows or storefronts in pink. Entry fees apply, and winners will be showcased on our website and social media platforms. More information will be released closer to date.
This event is not just about fun and celebration; it’s about making a real difference. Funds raised at Paint the Town Pink will support Beacon of Strength’s mission to provide essential resources, emotional support, and advocacy for individuals and families affected by breast cancer.
Stay tuned for more information and follow us on Facebook to stay updated and be notified when additional details are available.
